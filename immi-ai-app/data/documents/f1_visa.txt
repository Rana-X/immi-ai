F-1 Student Visa Information

The F-1 visa is for international students who wish to pursue academic studies in the United States. This visa category is for full-time students at an accredited college, university, seminary, conservatory, academic high school, or other academic institution.

Requirements:
1. Acceptance at a SEVP approved school
2. Proof of sufficient financial resources
3. Strong ties to home country
4. English language proficiency
5. Valid passport

Duration:
- Duration of your academic program
- Optional Practical Training (OPT) available for up to 12 months
- STEM OPT extension available for additional 24 months

Application Process:
1. Get accepted to a US school
2. Receive I-20 form
3. Pay SEVIS fee
4. Complete DS-160 form
5. Schedule visa interview
