H-1B Visa Overview

The H-1B visa is a non-immigrant visa that allows US companies to employ foreign workers in specialty occupations that require theoretical or technical expertise.

Key Features:
1. Dual Intent Visa
2. Initial stay of 3 years
3. Can be extended up to 6 years
4. Annual cap of 85,000 visas

Requirements:
1. Bachelor's degree or equivalent
2. Job offer from US employer
3. Position must be a specialty occupation
4. Employer must file LCA

Application Process:
1. Employer files LCA
2. Submit H-1B petition
3. If selected in lottery, USCIS processes petition
4. If approved, apply for visa at US consulate
